NATALIE TAN

ABOUT

Natalie is a Melbourne based medical doctor, training to become a full-stack developer. She enjoys creating practical applications which serve others.


During her career as a doctor, Natalie initially began programming in python as a hobby. Through what started off as a hobby, her passion for technology and her desire to learn continued to grow.


Enthralled by the endless possibilities through which technology can serve others, Natalie is now formally pursuing a Diploma of Information Technology through Australia’s Institute of Technology.


Outside of web development, Natalie has a strong interest in data. In her spare time, she enjoys scraping and processing data, whilst snacking on cheese and coffee.
